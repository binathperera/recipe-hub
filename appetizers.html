Appetizers

Bruschetta

Ingredients:

  • 4 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 baguette, sliced and toasted

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine tomatoes, garlic, basil, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  2. Let the mixture sit for at least 10 minutes to allow flavors to meld.
  3. Spoon the tomato mixture onto the toasted baguette slices.
  4. Serve immediately and enjoy!
